### **4. Content Direction**

**Topic Selection**

1.  **Product Recommendation/Sharing**: Recommend Wegic directly to your audience as something you've personally used, tested, and found valuable.
2.  **Product Review**: Provide an in-depth review of Wegic, sharing honest thoughts from multiple dimensions, including both pros and cons.
3.  **Tutorial Video**: Create an instructional video that guides your audience through the process of using Wegic, demonstrating its features and how to build a website step-by-step.
4.  **Focusing on New Product Features (Twitter Recommended)**: Highlight the latest features of Wegic in a concise and engaging manner, tailored for Twitter to keep your audience informed about updates and innovations.

**Content Specifications**

* **Content Types**:
    * Tiktok video
    * Instagram Reel
    * Instagram Carousel
    * YouTube video
    * Twitter thread
    * LinkedIn Article

* **Cover Size**:
    * Tiktok: 9:16, 1080p
    * Instagram Reel: 9:16, 1080p
    * Instagram Photo: 4:5, 1080p
    * YouTube: 16:9, 1080p
    * Twitter: 9:16, 1080p

* **Decorative Text on Cover**:
    * **Tiktok**: No specific requirements.
    * **Instagram**: For carousels, text should be visually appealing and highlight key words. For reels, it should contain essential information without overcrowding the cover.
    * **YouTube**: Use clear fonts, high contrast, and large, well-placed text. Highlight keywords like "Wegic" or "First AI web designer". Keep text concise and consistent with Wegic's brand identity.
    * **Twitter**: Something eye-catching and controversial will attract more attention.
    * **LinkedIn**: Professional, informative, and feature-driven posts highlighting benefits resonate best.

* **Title/Caption**:
    * **Keywords**: Must include phrases like "The first AI web designer, developer and manager", "Wegic", and "AI Team".
    * **Hashtags**: You must include #wegic / #wegicai. It is also recommended to incorporate them into popular and relevant hashtags like #aiteam, #webdesign, #ai, #aitech, etc., rather than using them alone.

* **Length**:
    * Tiktok video / Instagram Reel: 30 to 90 seconds.
    * YouTube video: 5 to 20 minutes.
    * Twitter thread: 1 main tweet + a minimum of 5 subtweets.
    * Instagram Carousel: Cover + a minimum of 5 pages.

* **Number of Deliverables**: 1-2 (Negotiable).
